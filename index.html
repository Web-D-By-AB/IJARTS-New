About IJARTS

International Journal of Advanced Research Trends in Science (IJARTS) is a peer reviewed research journal. The newly arrived journal will be spread and subscribed worldwide. The volumes and issues published in the research journal will explore the new horizon in the research sector of advaned applied science.

Aims and Objectives

To present and publish advanced research work in a high quality research journal like IJARTS with broader area of Applied Sciences

To select and publish high quality research manuscripts on science in the competitive world in a protective, peer reviewed and plagiarism free international journal like IJARTS.

To reach IJARTS as one of the trusted and valuable science research journal with world based impacts.

The journal covers the following areas of science

Animal and Veterinary Sciences, Fishery and Aquaculture Sciences, Agriculture and Forestry Sciences,Computer and Information Technology Sciences, Geography and Geological Sciences, Earth and Environmental Sciences, Engineering and Material Sciences, Medical and Forensic Sciences, Biological Sciences and Chemical Sciences
